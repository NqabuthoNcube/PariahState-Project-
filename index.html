Welcome to Pariah State – Zimbabwe’s Premier Urban Restaurant Destination

Nestled in the vibrant heart of Avondale, Harare, Pariah State has become a beloved landmark for locals and tourists alike. More than just a restaurant, it’s a lifestyle experience—where rustic charm meets modern culinary flair. Whether you’re meeting friends after work, enjoying a family dinner, or grabbing a quick bite on a busy day, Pariah State is the perfect spot to unwind and indulge.

FOOD OCEAN

Pariah State is a popular restaurant chain in Zimbabwe, with locations in Harare (Avondale, Borrowdale, Belgravia, and Riverside Walk) and Victoria Falls. It's known for its rustic, treehouse-style atmosphere and offers a variety of grilled food, pizzas, and unique dishes. Enhance your palate and experience a divine combination of flavour with us today!

Each Pariah State location offers a unique dining experience, blending modern culinary techniques with traditional Zimbabwean flavors. Whether you're enjoying a casual meal with friends or celebrating a special occasion, Pariah State provides a warm and inviting atmosphere. Our chefs are dedicated to crafting dishes that not only satisfy your hunger but also create lasting memories.

A Unique Atmosphere Inspired by Nature

Step into a space where treehouse-style architecture, warm timber tones, and glowing lanterns create a magical, forested ambiance right in the city. Our indoor-outdoor layout is adorned with hanging lights, leafy décor, and natural wooden finishes that immerse you in a relaxed and inviting setting. It’s no wonder Pariah State has earned a reputation as one of the best aesthetic restaurants in Harare.
